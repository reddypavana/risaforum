Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join RISA?
To join RISA, you can fill out the application form on our website, attend one of our open meetings, or contact us directly through this page. We welcome students from all disciplines!
Are there any membership fees?
RISA is primarily funded through university grants and sponsors. We have a small annual membership fee of $20 which helps cover costs for events and activities. However, fee waivers are available for students who need them.
How can my organization collaborate with RISA?
We're always open to collaborations with other student organizations, businesses, and community groups. Please contact us with your proposal, and our partnerships team will get back to you to discuss possibilities.
Can I volunteer for specific events only?
Absolutely! While we encourage consistent involvement, we understand that students have varying schedules. You can sign up as an event volunteer for specific activities that match your interests and availability.
